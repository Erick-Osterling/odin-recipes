Lasagna

This is meat and cheesse lasagna, italian style

Cooked with the finest ingredients it will make your guests imagine heaven

Don't leave this planet and incarnation without tasting this wonder food. You WILL NOT find anything similar or close to this in other realms

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • cheesse
  • Pasta
  • Oregano
  • Bechamel Sauce

Steps

  1. Add 200 grams of pasta
  2. Add Oregano
  3. Mix weel and add the rest of the ingredients
  4. Cook at 250 Celcius for 20 minutes or until it smeels like burned food
